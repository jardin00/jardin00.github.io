I am a master's student in the Department of Industrial and Management Engineering at Korea University, wherer I focus on mathematical optimization, AI algorithms, and heuristic algorithms for quantitative decision-making. Previously, I received my B.S. in Industrial and Systems Engineering from Dongguk University.

My research interests include optimization, and deep/reinforcement learning with applications to logistics and transportation systems.

### Methodology
- Mathematical optimization models & heuristic solution algorithms
- Development of artificial intelligence algorithms
- Quantitative decision-making & management insights

### Applications
- Robotic delivery services
- Logistics and Transportation systems
